Friender

The idea for Friender came from the isolation many experience when moving to a new place. While most platforms prioritize dating or superficial engagement, Friender takes a different approach: help people find their tribe and take the first step toward real-life connection — naturally, intelligently, and without friction. Here’s how it works: users don’t fill out long personality forms. Instead, Friender passively builds a detailed social and psychological profile using data from platforms users already use — like Spotify, Google Play Games, Instagram, and even ChatGPT history. These platforms provide insights into a user’s mood, energy, interests, and behavior patterns. Using this online fingerprint, Friender’s AI matching engine pairs users based on deep compatibility — not just shared hobbies, but how people think, communicate, and live.
